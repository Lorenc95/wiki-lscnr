# GameINFO

This quick reference explains common terms, abbreviations, and mechanics players encounter while playing. Read the short definition first, then check the Tips for practical advice.

## How to use this page
- Search for an abbreviation or term (e.g., "HP", "DPS").
- Read the definition and the Tips.

## Stats & Resources

- HP (Hit Points)
  - Meaning: Your character's health. At 0 you die or respawn.
  - Tips: Use potions or healing skills; avoid unnecessary damage.

- MP / Mana / EP
  - Meaning: Resource used to cast spells or special abilities.
  - Tips: Conserve for key moments; some items or skills restore it.

- Stamina
  - Meaning: Used for sprinting, dodging, or physical skills.
  - Tips: Running out usually prevents key defensive moves—manage during combat.

- XP (Experience Points)
  - Meaning: Earned from quests and combat; used to increase Level.
  - Tips: Prioritize missions that give the best XP-per-time if leveling fast matters.

- Level
  - Meaning: Measures character progression and unlocks new content.
  - Tips: Balance story and side quests to unlock important skills and gear.

## Combat Roles & Terms

- DPS (Damage Dealer)
  - Meaning: Focuses on dealing damage.
  - Tips: Maximize uptime on damage skills and target priority enemies.

- Tank
  - Meaning: Absorbs damage and holds enemy attention.
  - Tips: Use threat-generation abilities and defensive cooldowns.

- Healer
  - Meaning: Restores HP and prevents party wipe.
  - Tips: Track party HP and manage healing cooldowns carefully.

- Aggro / Threat
  - Meaning: Determines which player enemies attack.
  - Tips: Avoid pulling aggro if not tanking; tanks should use taunts when needed.

- CC (Crowd Control)
  - Meaning: Stuns, roots, silences, etc., that limit enemies.
  - Tips: Use CC to manage adds and interrupt dangerous mechanics.

- Buff / Debuff
  - Meaning: Positive effects on players / negative effects on enemies.
  - Tips: Maintain important buffs and remove harmful debuffs quickly.

## Items & Inventory

- Rarity tiers
  - Meaning: Common → Legendary; higher usually better.
  - Tips: Check stats before salvaging; some “rare” items may suit your build.

- Durability
  - Meaning: Gear wears and must be repaired.
  - Tips: Repair before difficult encounters to avoid penalties.

- Crafting
  - Meaning: Combine materials to make items.
  - Tips: Gather materials and check recipes; crafted items can be better than drops.

- Drop Rate / RNG
  - Meaning: Chance-based item drops or outcomes.
  - Tips: Expect variance—persistence or boosted difficulty/grouping often helps.

## World & Gameplay Systems

- NPC (Non-Player Character)
  - Meaning: In-game characters controlled by the game (quest givers, vendors).
  - Tips: Talk to NPCs often—some unlock content or provide important services.

- Quest types
  - Main / Story — progress plot
  - Side — optional rewards
  - Daily / Repeatable — resource/XP farming
  - Tips: Mix quest types for steady progression and rewards.

- Instance / Dungeon / Raid
  - Meaning: Group content with specific mechanics and rewards.
  - Tips: Learn mechanics before entering; coordinate with teammates.

- PvE / PvP
  - Meaning: Player vs Environment vs Player vs Player combat.
  - Tips: Optimize gear and tactics depending on mode.

- Respawn
  - Meaning: Returning to life after death; may have penalties.
  - Tips: Avoid risky behavior that causes frequent deaths.

## Mechanics & Shorthand

- Cooldown (CD)
  - Meaning: Time before a skill can be used again.
  - Tips: Plan rotations around key CDs for optimal damage or survivability.

- Scaling
  - Meaning: How difficulty or rewards change with level or group size.
  - Tips: Choose appropriate difficulty for best rewards relative to risk.

- Soft cap / Hard cap
  - Meaning: Soft = diminishing returns; Hard = absolute limit.
  - Tips: Reallocate stats once you hit diminishing returns.

## Communication & Community

- Guild / Clan
  - Meaning: Player-organized groups with social and gameplay benefits.
  - Tips: Good for finding regular groups and gaining support.

- LFG (Looking For Group)
  - Meaning: Tools/channels to find teammates for group content.
  - Tips: Use LFG for dungeons and raids when you don’t have a premade.

- Discord / Forums
  - Meaning: Community hubs for real-time help and announcements.
  - Tips: Join official/community channels to get live help and event info.
