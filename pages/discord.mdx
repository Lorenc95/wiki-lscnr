# Discord Rules

> By joining this Discord server, you agree to these rules. Common sense and respectful behavior are expected from all members. These rules are not exhaustive — using loopholes to violate the spirit of them is subject to enforcement.
>
> For appeals, reports, and general help, open a ticket in **#support-ticket**. You can report a Discord user or message by right-clicking or holding their message and selecting **Apps > Report to LSCnR**. Ping **@Staff** only if something requires immediate attention — mass spammers, scammers, or active griefers.

## 1. Hate Speech & Discrimination

Hate speech, slurs, and discriminatory language are strictly prohibited. This includes any remarks targeting someone's race, ethnicity, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, religion, or disability. Blasphemous remarks directed at religious groups are also not allowed.

---

## 2. Inappropriate Content

Posting or discussing graphic content, gore, violence, nudity, or pornography is not allowed. In-depth discussions of politics or religion are also not permitted, as these topics frequently lead to heated and unproductive arguments.

---

## 3. Advertising

Unsolicited references or links to other Discord servers, communities, games, services, or social media are not allowed — whether posted in chat or sent via direct message with the intent to advertise. Asking members to check your profile or DM you for promotional purposes also falls under this rule.

---

## 4. Spam & Channel Usage

Do not repeatedly send the same message, post irrelevant content, send unnecessary pings, or flood channels with duplicate images or GIFs. Read channel names and descriptions and use them for their intended purpose only. Use **#off-topic** for shitposting and casual conversation.

---

## 5. Private Information

Do not share, request, or discuss personal information about yourself or others that is not meant to be public. This includes real names, addresses, phone numbers, account details, and any internal server information or leaks.

---

## 6. English Only

English is the only permitted language in text and voice channels. This is to ensure fair and consistent moderation across the server. We may revisit international channels in the future as moderation capacity grows.

---

## 7. Tagging Staff

Do not ping staff members unless there is a genuine emergency requiring immediate action — such as an active mass spammer, scammer, or someone spawning objects on the server. For everything else, open a **#support-ticket**.

---

## 8. Asking for Staff

Do not ask to become staff, suggest yourself directly or indirectly, or hint at it. Doing so will negatively affect your chances of ever being considered.

Staff are selected internally based on server knowledge, performance, maturity, and other criteria evaluated through an internal process among senior staff. Asking does not help — it only reduces your chances.

---

## 9. Discord's Rules

In addition to these server-specific rules, the [Discord Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms) and [Discord Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines) apply to all members at all times.
