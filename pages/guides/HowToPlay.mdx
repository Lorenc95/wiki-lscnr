# Earning XP And Money

- # Robberies:
>You can gain XP And Money from robbing things like...
  - Banks, ATMS, Stores, Bank Trucks and Parking meters
  # Using the black market locations on the map<img width="30" height="30" alt="Image" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/o0pma/lscnr-imgaes/main/black%20market.png" />

The Black Market holds all things needed for Robbing Stores, Banks, ATM(s) and Parking Meters. They also carry items for other activities described further in this FAQ


Each item inside the black market has a max hold capacity. You can only buy and hold "x" amount of items on your person. 


Each item has its own value on how much you are allowed to hold.


Every item is 1 time use per use. if You have three and you use one. than you will have 2.

>selecting the items needed 
mostly Hacking laptops (*for banks*) Drills (*for banks and Advanced Store robberies* (cracking safes) Lock Picks (*cracking parking meters*)

# Store Robberies/Advanced Store Robberies<img width="30" height="30" alt="Image" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/o0pma/lscnr-imgaes/main/store.png" />
>there is a special requirement for advanced store robbing.

Locate a store you would like to rob on the minimap <img width="15" height="15" alt="Image" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/o0pma/lscnr-imgaes/main/store.png" />(only requirement for basic store robbing is to own atleast 1 gun)

### What Does A Store Look Like?
every store on the map will have an icon for it <img width="30" height="30" alt="Image" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/o0pma/lscnr-imgaes/main/store.png" /> 
locating that icon on the map and marking a waypoint will bring you directly to it.

>once you have located a store to rob you must enter the store and aim a fire arm at the clerk. there will be an intimidation bar that pops up. <img width="150" height="150" alt="Image" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/o0pma/lscnr-imgaes/main/intimidation%20bar.png" /> Once the intimidation bar reached max intimidation the robbery will be started.

<img width="150" height="150" alt="Image" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/o0pma/lscnr-imgaes/main/robbery%20start.PNG" /> 

NOTE: if you do not continue to aim ur fire arm at the store clerk the progression of the intimidation bar will decrease in wich you will fail the robbery and get a notifacation above the map  
<img width="150" height="150" alt="Image" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/o0pma/lscnr-imgaes/main/robbery%20failed.png" />

>after a while of aiming your fire arm at the store clerk. you will complete the robbery. in wich you will recive an amount of money (it varies) and a notifacation will pop up above the map notifying you and other players of a store robbery completion 

<img width="150" height="150" alt="Image" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/o0pma/lscnr-imgaes/main/Robberycompleted.PNG" />
